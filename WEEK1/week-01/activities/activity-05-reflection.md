# Activity 05: End-of-Class Reflection

## Purpose

Give students time to review what they learned, identify confusion and connect HTML to real websites.

## Time

10 minutes

## Individual Reflection

Answer the questions below in your own words.

1. What did you learn today?
2. What confused you?
3. What is the difference between the Internet and the Web?
4. Where do you see HTML in real life?
5. Which websites or apps do you now look at differently?
6. What would you like to build with HTML, CSS and JavaScript?
7. How could GitHub help you as a student developer?

## Pair Discussion

Share one answer with a partner:

- One thing you understand better now
- One thing you still want explained again
- One idea for your portfolio page

## Exit Ticket

Before leaving, complete these two sentences:

- Today I learned that...
- Next week I want to understand...
